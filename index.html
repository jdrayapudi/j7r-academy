Institutional Profile

Building AI Capacity in Healthcare

Our Mission

J7R Academy is an independent AI education and training organisation based out of Gurugram, dedicated to building AI capacity in healthcare—from foundational awareness to applied expertise.

We design and deliver programmes for clinicians, faculty, administrators, and healthcare teams navigating the AI era, ensuring responsible and effective adoption.

Scope of Training Offerings

AI Literacy & Awareness

Foundational programmes for clinical and non-clinical healthcare teams.

Applied AI in Healthcare

Hands-on training designed specifically for clinicians and medical educators.

AI Governance & Ethics

Regulatory frameworks and strategic planning for hospital leadership and administrators.

Faculty & Executive Development

AI-enabled teaching tools for educators and AI strategy for senior healthcare professionals.
